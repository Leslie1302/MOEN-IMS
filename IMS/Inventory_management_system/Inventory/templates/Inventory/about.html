⚡ MOEGT IEPS

The Integrated Energy Project System (IEPS) is the foundational digital platform driving the Ministry of Energy and Green Transition's logistical excellence and infrastructure deployment.

Our Mission

To accelerate the deployment of energy infrastructure across Ghana through precise inventory tracking, transparent resource management, and optimized logistics workflows.

The MOEGT IEPS emerged from a critical need to bridge the gap between ministerial authorizations and field-level implementation. By integrating Bill of Quantities (BoQ) tracking with real-time warehouse movements, we ensure that every pole, transformer, and meter is accounted for from delivery to installation.

System Architecture

Drawdown Guardrails

Prevents material releases that exceed ministerial authorizations through the Release Letter system.

BoQ Analysis

Dynamic monitoring of Bill of Quantities to identify and justify project overissuances in real-time.

Logistics Loop

End-to-end transport tracking from warehouse release to consultant-verified site receipt.

Automated Alerts

Intelligent notifications for low stock, new requests, and transport milestones.
