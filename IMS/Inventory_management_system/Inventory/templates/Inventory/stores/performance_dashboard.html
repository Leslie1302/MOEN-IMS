How Store Officer Performance is Graded
Performance Score = Completion (40%) + Efficiency (30%) + Volume (30%)
A+ (90-100) A (85-89) B+ (80-84) B (75-79) C+ (70-74) C (65-69) D (60-64) F (<60)< /span>
Completion Rate (40%)

Percentage of assigned orders successfully completed and marked as "Completed" status.

Efficiency (30%)

Average time from assignment to completion:
• <2 days = Excellent (30 pts)
• 2-5 days = Good (20 pts)
• 5-10 days = Average (10 pts)
• >10 days = Needs improvement (0 pts)

Volume/Productivity (30%)

Total completed orders:
• ≥50 = High (30 pts)
• 30-49 = Good (25 pts)
• 10-29 = Moderate (15 pts)
• 1-9 = Low (5 pts)
