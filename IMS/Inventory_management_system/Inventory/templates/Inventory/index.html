About the System

The **MOEGT Integrated Energy Project System (IEPS)** is designed to help the Ministry efficiently track energy projects, stock levels, manage material requests, and streamline procurement processes. With real-time updates and user-friendly features, you’ll always stay informed about your project and inventory status.

Whether you're handling warehouse supplies or office resources, this system ensures smooth and hassle-free inventory control across all regions.
