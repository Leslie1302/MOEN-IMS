Help Center

Getting Started

MOEGT IEPS (Integrated Energy Project System) is a role-based system used to manage material requests, store operations, transport, and site receipts across MOEGT energy projects.

Key Areas in the Navbar

  • 📊 Dashboard: Overview of stock levels, recent activity, and performance indicators.
  • 📋 Schedule: For schedule officers and management to create and track material requests.
  • 🏪 Store Operations: For storekeepers to process orders and manage transporter assignments.
  • 👔 Management: Management dashboards, performance reports, and system notifications.
  • 🏗️ Projects: Access Bill of Quantity (BoQ) and overissuance summaries (for authorized users).
  • 🔔 Notifications: Recent system alerts related to your role (requests, processing, transport, receipts, BOQ updates, etc.).
  • 👤 User Menu: Profile settings, Help Center, and Sign Out.

How the System Works by Role

Schedule Officers

  • Create material requests from the Request Material page.
  • Track progress from request to delivery via Material Orders and Site Receipts.
  • Use notifications and dashboards to monitor pending or delayed requests.

Storekeepers

  • View assigned work from Store Operations → My Assigned Orders.
  • Process and release materials, then update Transport Status.
  • Monitor performance from the Storekeeper Performance Dashboard.

Transport & Site Teams

  • Transporters update delivery progress via Transportation Status.
  • Consultants or site officers log deliveries using Site Receipts.

Management

  • Use the Management Dashboard to review staff performance and system activity.
  • Review BoQ overissuance summaries and justifications where applicable.
  • Receive high-level alerts through the notification center.

Notifications, Profile & Security

Notifications

  • Click the 🔔 bell in the navbar to see recent alerts related to your role.
  • Select a notification to view full details or open the full Notifications page.
  • Use Mark All as Read to clear old alerts once reviewed.

Profile Settings

  • Open the user menu in the top-right and click Profile settings.
  • Update your name, email, and profile picture where allowed.
  • Use the Change Password section on the profile page to update your password securely.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

  • If enabled for your account, follow the on-screen steps on the 2FA setup pages.
  • Store backup codes safely; they allow access if you lose your primary device.

Troubleshooting & Contact Support

If you experience issues, try the following first:

  • Refresh the page and ensure you are still signed in.
  • Use a modern browser (latest Chrome/Edge/Firefox) and disable outdated extensions that modify pages.
  • Clear your browser cache if tables or dashboards do not load correctly.
  • Check your network connection, especially for large downloads (e.g., reports, waybills).
