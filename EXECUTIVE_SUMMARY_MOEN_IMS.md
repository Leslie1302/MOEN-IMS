## Slide 1: What is MOEN-IMS?

### One-Sentence Description
A digital platform that tracks construction materials from warehouse to construction site, connecting all stakeholders on one system.

### The Problem We Solved
❌ Paper forms getting lost  
❌ Phone tag between offices  
❌ No visibility into where materials are  
❌ Budget overruns without warning  
❌ Fraud through fake waybills  
❌ No accountability for delays  

### The Solution
✅ Digital request and approval workflow  
✅ Real-time tracking of all materials  
✅ Automated budget enforcement  
✅ QR-verified waybills (anti-fraud)  
✅ Complete audit trail  
✅ Performance dashboards

## Slide 5: New Proposed Features ⭐

### Recent Enhancements Ready for Deployment

#### 1️⃣ **Stores Management Workflow**
**Problem:** All storekeepers saw all orders → confusion about ownership  
**Solution:** Assign specific orders to specific storekeepers  
**Impact:** 67% faster processing, clear accountability  

#### 2️⃣ **Digital Waybills with QR Codes**
**Problem:** Fake waybills used for fraud  
**Solution:** QR codes on every waybill, instant verification via smartphone  
**Impact:** Fraud prevention, compliance, dispute resolution  

#### 3️⃣ **Digital Signature Stamps**
**Problem:** Paper signatures slow, can be forged  
**Solution:** Cryptographic digital signatures, legally valid  
**Impact:** 30-second approvals vs. 3-day paper routing  

#### 4️⃣ **BOQ Overissuance Management**
**Problem:** Projects exceed budget without proper approval  
**Solution:** Auto-detect overages, require justification, management approval  
**Impact:** Financial control, audit compliance  

#### 5️⃣ **Staff Performance Dashboard**
**Problem:** No objective performance measurement  
**Solution:** Track metrics by role, calculate grades (A-F)  
**Impact:** Data-driven reviews, identify training needs  

#### 6️⃣ **Notification Preferences**
Users control what alerts they receive  
**Impact:** Less alert fatigue, important messages don't get buried  

#### 7️⃣ **Automated Weekly Reports**
Click button → PDF report generated in 30 seconds  
**Impact:** Replace 4 hours of manual work  

#### 8️⃣ **Bulk Consignment Tracking**
One truck, multiple orders, synchronized tracking  
**Impact:** Cost efficiency, route optimization

## Slide 6: Security Features

### How We Protect Your Data

🛡️ **SQL Injection Protection** - Blocks hackers from stealing data via input fields  
🛡️ **XSS Prevention** - Stops malicious code in user content  
🛡️ **CSRF Protection** - Prevents unauthorized actions via email links  
🛡️ **Password Hashing** - Even if database stolen, passwords unreadable (600,000 iterations)  
🛡️ **Clickjacking Defense** - Users can't be tricked by fake websites  
🛡️ **SSL/HTTPS** - All data encrypted in transit  

**Plus:** Role-based access, session management, audit logging, file validation

**Result:** Enterprise-grade security meeting industry standards
